Professional Equipment

Our studio is equipped with professional-grade equipment to ensure the highest quality audio production. We feature Digidesign's ProTools HD recording software and hardware, Neumann U87 microphones, Sennheiser boom and lapel microphones, and Neve mic preamps. Our facility includes a 42" plasma screen for ADR sessions, comfortable seating, versatile track lighting, and private off-street parking for your convenience.
