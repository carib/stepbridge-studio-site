Studio Location

Stepbridge Studios is located in the heart of the Santa Fe Historic Guadalupe District, just a few minutes walk from the Plaza.

Address

Stepbridge Studios

Parking Information

  • Private off-street parking available
  • Street parking available on surrounding blocks
  • Accessible parking spaces available

Getting Here

From Santa Fe Plaza

  • Head south on Guadalupe Street
  • Turn right onto Jose Street
  • Studio is located on the right side

Public Transportation

  • Santa Fe Trails bus service available
  • Nearest bus stop: Guadalupe & Jose Streets
