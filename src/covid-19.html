COVID-19 Safety Protocols

Stepbridge Studios is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all clients and staff. We continue to follow recommended health and safety guidelines.

Current Safety Measures

  • Enhanced cleaning and sanitization protocols
  • Air filtration and ventilation systems
  • Sanitizing stations available throughout the studio
  • Regular health screenings for staff

Remote Session Options

  • Remote file delivery options
